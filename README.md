## We create new value.

We bring together creative, data and technology talent with production and procurement expertise, to improve both marketing performance and production efficiencies for brands.

We unlock growth for our clients by helping them deliver a more engaging customer experience.

* **Data insight** – Commercialise insights to create value from data
* **Strategy and creative** – Create experiences that are as rewarding for consumers as they are for brands
* **Print management** – Deliver sustainable and efficient global print solutions
* **POS design and production** – Engage consumers in store efficiently and effectively
* **Creative Production** – Reduce marketing production friction to be faster to market
* **Marketing technology** – Orchestrate and automate martech to create new value
* **Digital channel activation** – Be there for your consumers through email, DM, social, VoD and more
* **Promotions and merchandise** – Connect with consumers in tangible ways with merch and products

Indicia Worldwide is the product of three successful businesses, brought together under our parent company [Konica Minolta](https://www.konicaminolta.com/). The vision was, and is, to create new value across the remit of marketing for our clients.
